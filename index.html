Your Therapist

Hi, I'm Jordan

I'm a licensed massage therapist based in St Petersburg, FL, with specialized training across a range of modalities — from Swedish and deep tissue to oncology massage, lymphatic drainage, and reflexology. That breadth means I can meet you where you actually are, not just where a session type says you should be.

Some clients come to me burned out and needing to simply switch off. Others are athletes recovering from a race, people managing chronic pain, or those navigating the physical effects of cancer treatment. What I love about this work is that every session starts with listening — to your body, your history, and what the day actually calls for.

My approach is calm, attentive, and grounded in the understanding that healing isn't always linear. Some sessions release a lot. Some sessions don't — and that's okay too. The body knows what it needs, and my job is to create the conditions where it can do that safely.

— Jordan
