Policies & FAQ

Everything you need to know before your visit. We're here to make your experience seamless and comfortable.

💰 Deposits

To secure your appointment, a credit card is required at the time of booking. A small deposit will be charged to hold your time. This deposit goes toward your treatment.

  • Standard appointments (60+ min) $30 deposit
  • Shorter sessions (30-45 min) $20 deposit
  • Specialty services Varies by service

📅 Cancellation Policy

We understand that life happens. Our policy is designed to be fair while ensuring we can serve all our clients.

  • 24+ hours before Full refund, no fee
  • 12-24 hours before Deposit forfeited, no additional charge
  • Under 12 hours Full charge applied
  • No-call/no-show Full service fee charged

⏰ Late Arrivals

We value your time and strive to start every appointment on schedule. Here's how we handle late arrivals:

  • Up to 15 min late Session shortened, full price
  • 15+ min late May need to reschedule
  • Note We cannot extend sessions past original end time

🚫 No-Shows

If you don't arrive for your scheduled appointment without contacting us, the following applies:

  • First no-show Full service charge + future deposit required
  • Repeat no-shows May require prepayment for future bookings

🔄 Rescheduling

Need to change your appointment? Here's how:

  • Online rescheduling Available up to 12 hours before
  • By phone/text Happy to help anytime
  • Multiple changes May require new deposit

💳 Payment Methods

We accept a variety of payment options for your convenience:

  • Credit/debit cards Visa, Mastercard, Amex
  • Digital payments Venmo, PayPal
  • At studio Card on file or mobile payment
  • Tipping Not required, but appreciated

We Honor Your Health

If you're feeling unwell, please let us know. We're happy to reschedule or offer a credit toward a future visit. Your wellbeing—and that of our community—comes first.

📋 Intake Forms

For your safety and best experience, we ask that you complete a brief health intake form before your first visit. This helps us:

  • Understand your health history Including any conditions or injuries
  • Personalize your treatment To your specific needs and preferences
  • Ensure safe practices Adapted to your comfort level
  • Form completed Online, takes 5 minutes

💜 Health & Wellness Considerations

We welcome everyone and are trained to adapt our treatments to various health situations. Please let us know if you have:

  • Chronic conditions Diabetes, heart conditions, etc.
  • Recent injuries or surgeries We'll modify as needed
  • Pregnancy We have pregnancy-safe options
  • Allergies Lotions, oils, scents
  • Sensitive skin We have fragrance-free options

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I expect at my first appointment?

Arrive 10-15 minutes early. You'll complete a brief health intake form, then meet your therapist for a quick consultation. After that, enjoy your personalized treatment. We'll always ensure you're comfortable.

Which service is right for me?

If you're new or want relaxation, start with Light Touch Massage. For specific tension, try Deep Tissue or Therapeutic Massage. Not sure? Check our "Not Sure What to Book?" section on the Services page, or simply tell us what you're feeling and we'll recommend.

Are deposits required?

Yes, a small deposit is required to secure your booking. This ensures your time is reserved for you. The deposit goes toward your final treatment cost. We understand plans change—just give us 24 hours notice for a full refund.

Can I reschedule online?

Yes! You can reschedule through our booking system up to 12 hours before your appointment. After that, please call or text us. We're happy to help with any changes.

How should I prepare for my massage?

Wear comfortable clothing. Come hydrated but not starving. Arrive a bit early to settle in. Most importantly—relax! We'll handle the rest. Let your therapist know your preferences and any areas you want focused on (or avoided).

Do you offer support for stress relief and sleep?

Absolutely. Our Deep Sleep Massage and Light Touch Massage are specifically designed for stress relief and nervous system support. We also offer guidance on relaxation techniques you can use at home.

What is lymphatic drainage?

Lymphatic drainage is a gentle technique that encourages the lymph system to release toxins and reduce fluid retention. It's incredibly soothing, supports the immune system, and can help with swelling, digestion, and overall detoxification.

Is oncology massage gentle and supportive?

Yes. Our oncology massage is specifically adapted for clients going through or recovering from cancer treatment. We use gentle pressure, avoid certain areas, and tailor every session to your current health and comfort level. Your safety and comfort are our priority.

Can I add extra time to my session?

Yes! Our "30 Minute Add On" lets you extend any appointment by 30 minutes. Simply select it when booking, or ask your therapist during your visit if time allows. It's perfect for adding extra focus on problem areas.

Questions? We're Here to Help

Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out and we'll be happy to assist.
