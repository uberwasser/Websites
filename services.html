Frequently Asked Questions

What should I expect at my first visit?

Plan to arrive early. You'll fill out a brief health form, meet your therapist, discuss your needs, then enjoy your customized treatment.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations or changes. Late cancellations may incur a fee.

Do I need to tip?

Gratuity is not required but appreciated if you're happy with your service. It's entirely your choice.

What if I have health concerns?

Please mention any health conditions when booking. We will discuss any adaptations needed during your consultation.
