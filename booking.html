Payments & Deposits

💰 Deposit Required

A small deposit secures your appointment. First-time clients: $30. Returning clients: $20 for standard sessions.

🔒 Secure Checkout

All payments are processed securely through encrypted connections. We never store your full card details.

💳 Card on File

We keep a card on file for convenience. It will only be charged for late cancellations or no-shows per our policy.

❓ Cancellations

24+ hours: Full refund. 12-24 hours: Deposit forfeited. Under 12 hours: Full charge. See our Policies page for details.

Your deposit goes toward your treatment

The deposit amount is applied to your final bill—you're only paying for your session, not extra fees.

Quick Answers

Can I reschedule online?

Yes! Reschedule through your booking confirmation up to 12 hours before. After that, please call or text us.

What is your cancellation policy?

24+ hours: Full refund. 12-24 hours: Deposit forfeited. Under 12 hours: Full service charge. Please see Policies page.

What should I expect at my first visit?

Arrive early, complete a brief intake form, meet your therapist, discuss your needs, then enjoy your customized treatment.

Is my payment secure?

Absolutely. We use encrypted payment processing through trusted booking platforms. Your data is safe.
